Group 9

Group 9 (G9) are a group of game developers currently working on a new shopping simulator game. CoCost Simulator is a game developed to help promote the tech side of Costco that goes unrecognised due to the variety of wholesale foods.

G9 is a group of four student from Greenwich University.
