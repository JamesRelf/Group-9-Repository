CoCost Simulator is a shopping simulator game set in Costco, where the customer can buy items ranging from food to electronics and so we aim to promote the electronics side of CoCost’s business.​ The player is given a shopping list and must search for and purchase all the products on the shopping list to complete the level; this must be done within a time limit. ​ There are multiple versions of products that include a cheap version and an expensive version. Therefore, the player must decide whether a more expensive version of a product is worth the cost. ​ Furthermore, the player might encounter angry customers and must do their best to avoid being attacked.​ CoCost Simulator aims to promote CoCost to a gamer-oriented audience, as well as promoting the CoCost membership cards. ​ To achieve this, the game will be sold with membership discount codes and promote an authentic CoCost shopping experience.
