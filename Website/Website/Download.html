CoCost Simulator

CoCost Simulator is a first person shopping simulator developed by Group 9 to help the technology side of Costco gain more attention as it goes underappreciated due to the variety of products they sell. CoCost Simulator gives the user a shopping list to complete within a specified budget and time.
